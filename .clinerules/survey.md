# Paddle Up League Interest Survey — Draft Questions

Intro (1–2 lines)
- We’re testing interest in a competitive, weekly, rotating-partner pickleball league in St. Louis. The league is merit-based with promotion/relegation, weekly standings, and season prizes. This survey takes ~3–5 minutes.

---

## Respondent details
1. Name (optional) — Short text  
2. Email (optional) — Email  
3. DUPR rating (required) — Numeric / short text (e.g., 3.85)  
4. How long have you played organized pickleball? — Single choice: `<1 year`, `1–2 years`, `3–5 years`, `5+ years`  
5. How would you describe your competitive level? — Single choice: `Casual`, `Competitive`, `Pro-am`  

---

## Overall interest
6. How interested are you in joining this weekly competitive league? — Likert: `Very interested` / `Somewhat interested` / `Neutral` / `Unlikely` / `Not interested`  
7. Would you commit to attending most weekly nights for a 6–8 week season? — Single choice: `Yes` / `Maybe` / `No`  
8. Preferred season length — Single choice: `6 weeks` / `8 weeks` / `No preference`  

---

## Scheduling & logistics
9. The league runs Sundays, 7:00–10:00 PM. Does that work for you? — `Yes` / `Sometimes` / `No` (optional text field for comments)  
10. How far would you travel for league nights? — Single choice: `<10 min`, `10–25 min`, `25–45 min`, `45+ min`  
11. How many nights per month can you reliably attend? — Numeric or range (e.g., `0–1`, `2–3`, `4+`)  

---

## Format & play preferences
12. Are you comfortable with rotating partners (individual performance tracked)? — `Yes` / `No` / `Prefer mostly fixed partners` (optional comment)  
13. Do you prefer promotion/relegation (courts move by performance) or static grouping? — `Promotion/relegation` / `Static groups` / `No preference`  
14. Are rally scoring and first-to-11 acceptable? — `Yes` / `No` / `Comments`  
15. Would you prefer 3 rounds (with court promotion) each night or a different structure? — Short text / multiple choice (Provide options if you want)

---

## Attendance, subs & fairness
16. Which attendance policy do you prefer? — Single choice:  
- `Zero tolerance (miss = 0 points)`  
- `1 grace week per season allowed (excused)`  
- `Scaled penalty / makeup options`  
17. Sub policy preference — Single choice:  
- `Admin assigns subs only (recommended)`  
- `Players may request a sub; admin approves`  
- `Players may bring a sub who counts toward their points`  
18. How strict should DUPR matching be for subs? — Single choice: `Strict (±0.10)` / `Moderate (±0.20)` / `Loose (±0.50)` / `No preference`  
19. Would you volunteer to sub or help with league operations? — `Yes` / `Maybe` / `No`  

---

## Prizes & incentives
20. Which rewards matter most to you for season finishers? (Choose all that apply) — `Court credit`, `Cash`, `Free entry next season`, `Club points`, `Merchandise`, `Recognition / featured match`  
21. How important are each for top-3 finishers? — Grid / Likert: `Very important` / `Somewhat important` / `Not important` for: `Court credit`, `Free entry`, `Club points`, `Trophy / recognition`  
22. Would bonus points (e.g., +10 for Championship Court win, +10 for undefeated night) change your interest? — `Yes` / `No` / `Maybe` (optional: which matters more)  

---

## Pricing & commitment
23. What entry fee would you pay per season? — Single choice: `$0`, `$25`, `$50`, `$75`, `$100+` (adjust as you prefer)  
24. Would prize structure (court credit / free entry) affect willingness to pay? — `Yes` / `No` / `Maybe` (optional comment)  

---

## Motivations & profile
25. What is your primary reason to join? — Single choice: `Competitive play`, `Improve ranking`, `Meet players`, `Practice`, `Other (text)`  
26. How often do you play outside this league currently? — Single choice: `0–1 per week`, `2–3 per week`, `4+ per week`  

---

## Open feedback
27. What would make you more likely to join? — Long text  
28. Any concerns or non-negotiables we should know? — Long text  
29. Preferred prizes or alternate rewards not listed above — Long text

---

## Optional demographic filters (helpful for analysis)
- Age range — Single choice  
- Gender — Optional  
- Preferred communication channel — Single choice: `Email`, `Text`, `Phone`, `DM`

---

## Form design tips
- Make DUPR, interest level, availability (time + travel), and attendance preference required fields.  
- Use conditional questions: show sub-policy follow-ups only if respondent indicates they might bring or prefer subs.  
- Keep total time under 10 minutes. Prioritize required items early.

---

## Invitation copy (short)
Subject: Quick poll — Competitive Sunday pickleball league (2–3 min)

Body:  
We’re exploring a competitive, rotating-partner pickleball league in St. Louis (Sundays 7–10 PM). If you play regularly and want higher-level weekly competition, please fill this 3–5 minute survey so we can design the league around player preferences. [Survey link] — Thanks!
